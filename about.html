Our Story

Cricket Citadel is built by cricket lovers, for cricket lovers. This is your stronghold of all things cricket.

Founded in 2025, Cricket Citadel is a passion-driven platform dedicated to delivering the most authentic, insightful, and elite-level cricket content. From local grounds to international arenas, our aim is to be the definitive hub for everything cricket.

Our journey began with a vision: to build a citadel of knowledge and resources for players, fans, and experts. We’re continuously evolving — with content, gear, and insights that empower cricketers at every level.
